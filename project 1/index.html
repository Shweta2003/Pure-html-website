My name is Shweta Mandal.

I have two siblings among them one is my sister and the second one is my younger brother.
I am the first child in my family so from the earlier days i got a lot of love and support from all the family members.
I am so much a favorite of my grandparents and most of my childhood time I spent with them in the village.

I had started my primary education in village school, That days with so many memorable incidences still in my mind.
Though that school was so simple but basic education and values i had learnt from this school.
Almost all types of childhood games i played that time, where i had so many friends too.
I didn’t know where the day ended and our naughty activities and games were never going to end.

Up to the class 7th education i have completed from the village school, and my father was so much insistent
about admitting me to town school for my next education. Though it was not an easy decision for me to
move on in new place and new school by leaving my grandparents, village school and friends. But my school
teacher was always advising me one thing that is,” If you want to achieve something, You have to be ready for sacrifice something”.

My teachers words somewhere convinced me and i had accepted their suggestion, but from the bottom of my
heart i was emotional on that day when i left my village and grandparents’ home. And with my parents,
I had moved on to the town where we have already a home.

That time everything was new for me, where i didn’t know much more about town. But before starting new school
I had traveled to most of the locations in town with my parents and some places was well known to me soon after some days.

I was good in study and since childhood my dream was about to become a mechanical engineer, so from class

library because reading is my favorite hobby since childhood. With new friends and school days i didn’t
realized how two years passed on and i got admitted in class 10th.

I knew very well this three years are so crucial in my career that I must give my best efforts so that
only I will be able for engineering admission. Meanwhile, I was missing my village and old friends,
so during summer vacation, I was always going to meet them in the village.

I started daily six hours of study in class on the 10th my target was above 90 percent anyhow about to score.
And in the last two months before final exam of class 10th, with very honest efforts and dedication, I studied hard.
As per my expectations exam was good and happily i solved all the questions. So final result was according to that
and I had achieved 96 percent marks, Main important thing was my mathematics and science subjects marks, where I scored out of marks.
